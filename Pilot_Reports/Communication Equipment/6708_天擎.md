Mars Semiconductor Corp. operates as a long-distance high-bandwidth wireless audio and video chip design company in Taiwan. It offers wireless home, car, and baby monitors; and electronic components, as well as SaaS one-stop cloud application service. Mars Semiconductor Corp. was founded in 2001 and is headquartered in Hsinchu City, Taiwan.
