## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Communication Equipment
**市值:** 1,699 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 862 百萬台幣

Comtrend Corporation engages in the research, manufacture, marketing, and maintenance of broadband communication equipment Taiwan, the United States, Europe, and internationally. It offers cable and wireless transmission equipment, multiplexers, digital subscriber lines and loop carrier systems, synchronous optical network equipment, and synchronous cable accessories. The company also offers extenders, switches, G.hn powerline and coax adapters, fiber, VDSL, home networking, coax MDU/CPE, ACS, powerline module solutions, GPON, XGS-PON, one GE port and home gateway, wireless routers, PoE devices, and security surveillance systems. Comtrend Corporation was incorporated in 1990 and is headquartered in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
