## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Communication Equipment
**市值:** 3,501 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 3,195 百萬台幣

ATW Technology Inc. engages in the processing, manufacturing, and trading of communications and network electronic products in Taiwan. It offers VDSL routers, GPON routers, Wi-Fi and mesh routers, Wi-Fi USB adapters, and VOIP gateway enterprise solutions; smart [[IoT]] products; 4G/4.[[5G]] wireless communication products, as well as wireless indoor and outdoor AP and repeaters, wireless indoor network adapters, networking adapters, and boosters; access switch products; set-top boxes; and solid-state hydrogen energy solutions. The company was formerly known as L&K Precision Technology Co., Ltd. and changed its name to ATW Technology Inc. in September 2013. ATW Technology Inc. was founded in 1986 and is headquartered in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
