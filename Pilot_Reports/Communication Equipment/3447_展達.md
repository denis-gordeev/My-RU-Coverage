## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Communication Equipment
**市值:** 3,125 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 2,505 百萬台幣

XAVi Technologies Corporation, together with its subsidiaries, engages in the research, development, manufacture, and sale of networking communication products in Taiwan and Asia. The company offers broadband access products, including active ethernet, GPON, G.fast 212 single port DPU, and G.fast 106/212 CPE; short and middle range internet of things products for commercial, industrial, consumer, human to machine, and machine to machine applications; wifi access point and wi-fi PTP/PMP products; and multimedia products. It is also involved in overseas investment activities. The company serves the telecommunication, enterprise, small business, SOHO, and family and general household networking markets. XAVi Technologies Corporation was incorporated in 1997 and is headquartered in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
