## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Communication Equipment
**市值:** 746 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 471 百萬台幣

Z-Com, Inc. engages in the research, development, designing, manufacturing, and sale of software, hardware, and system integration for wireless communication systems Taiwan, Germany, Mainland China, and internationally. The company provides outdoor access points, serial device servers, industrial cellular gateway, cellular gateway, Wi-fi module, and accessories. It also offers new energy fields and communication management, private network, surveillance, wireless hotels, in-vehicle Wi-fi, smart campus, carrier-grade Wi-fi, and rural Wi-fi solutions. In addition, The company provides zMEC edge, a computing platform; and ZOAM, an operation administration maintenance system. Further, it offers modem and communication electronics products and modems. The company was founded in 1995 and is headquartered in Hsinchu City, Taiwan.
