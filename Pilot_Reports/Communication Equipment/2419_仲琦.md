## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology

**企業價值:** 9,030 百萬台幣

Hitron Technologies Inc., together with its subsidiaries, offers broadband access networking products and solutions in Taiwan, America, Europe, and Asia. It operates through System Integration and Manufacturing segments. The company offers [[5G]]; cable modems and gateway; fiber ONT/ONUs; wireless networking; and coax network testing tools. It also provides home network management solutions, such as HitronCloud, a cloud-based platform that provides real time visibility to the health of the IP network, HFC plant, in-home wiring, Wi-Fi, and health of the CPE hardware/firmware connected to the home network; OptiMy service manages and optimizes residential Wi-Fi home networks; as well as MyHitron+ app, which provides multi-location management that enables customers to remotely manage multiple locations. Hitron Technologies Inc. was incorporated in 1986 and is based in Hsinchu City, Taiwan. Hitron Technologies Inc. is a subsidiary of Alpha Networks Inc.
