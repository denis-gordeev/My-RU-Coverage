Arima Communications Corp. engages in the manufacturing, processing, and sale of mobile phones, electronic components, and solar products and equipment in Taiwan. It offers enterprise display platform, mobile devices, and smart sensing and parking solutions. The company also manufactures and sells solar energy products and equipment. In addition, it is involved in provision of marketing, international trade, and product design services; and manufacture of communication and machinery equipment. Arima Communications Corp. was incorporated in 1999 and is based in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
