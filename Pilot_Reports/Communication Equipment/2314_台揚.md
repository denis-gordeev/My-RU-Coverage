## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Communication Equipment
**市值:** 916 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 3,732 百萬台幣

Microelectronics Technology Inc. designs, manufactures, and sells terrestrial microwave, satellite communication system, and customized products in the United States, Mainland China, and internationally. The company offers satellite broadband solutions, including C, Ku and Ka-band VSAT Transceivers/ODUs/BUCs/LNBs, and VSAT flat panel antenna modules for GEO and LEO applications, as well as satellite in-door unit. It also provides low noise block downconverter (LNB), such as multi feed, digital channel stack switch, and universal LNB series; mobile communication products comprising 4G/[[5G]] RU and cellular backhaul; and RFID solutions which includes reader SiP and SiP reader/module. Microelectronics Technology Inc. was incorporated in 1983 and is headquartered in Hsinchu City, Taiwan.
