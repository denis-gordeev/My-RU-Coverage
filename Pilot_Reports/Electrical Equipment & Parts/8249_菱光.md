# 8249 - 菱光

## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Industrials
**產業:** Electrical Equipment & Parts
**市值:** 8,396 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 6,443 百萬台幣

Creative Sensor Inc. manufactures and trades in image sensors and its electronic components in China, Thailand, the Philippines, and internationally. It offers contact image sensor modules for image scanning applications; stealth laser dicing services for use in various MEMS, RFID, and image sensors; and LWIR products, including uncooled infra-red thermal imaging modules/cores used in various areas, such as security surveillance, drone, transportation, temperature measurement, and national security. The company was founded in 1998 and is based in Taipei, Taiwan.
