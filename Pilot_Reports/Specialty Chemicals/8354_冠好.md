## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Basic Materials
**產業:** Specialty Chemicals
**市值:** 1,209 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 2,281 百萬台幣

Koan Hao Technology Co., Ltd. engaged in the processing, manufacturing, and trading of trademark paper, release paper, tape and other products in Taiwan, Vietnam, China, and internationally. The company offers release liners and adhesive tapes. It also provides pressure sensitive labels, including water-based, solvent-based, hot melt type, etc. for commodity packaging and industrial purposes; release papers, release films, and PE coated products; and polyimide protective films, polyester protective films, and OPP lamination tapes/transfer tapes. In addition, the company offers industrial double-sided, electronics, and quad flat no-leads package tapes; and specialty tapes, including fiberglass tapes, UV, thermal release tapes, cooling stripping type protective films, and lithographic red PET tapes. The company was formerly known as Koan Hao Enterprise Co., Ltd. and changed its name to Koan Hao Technology Co., Ltd. in 2013. Koan Hao Technology Co., Ltd. was founded in 1981 and is based in Nantou City, Taiwan.
