## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Basic Materials

Nan Ya Plastics Corporation engages in the production and sale of plastic processed products, polyester fibers, chemical products, and electronic materials in Taiwan, China and Hong Kong, the United States, and internationally. It provides plastics products, including PP synthetic paper, adhesive, optical film, outdoor decoration materials, TPU film, synthetic leather, PU resin, doors, Neuma fire-rated door and windows, plastic pallets, PVC cling film, PE cling film, BOPP film, engineering and PVC compounds, unsaturated polyester, pipe and fitting, rigid sheet, A-PET sheet/rigid film, interior decoration materials, PVC sheets, PP synthetic paper, PVC rigid sheet, injection, decoration film, window film, PET film, and PU-DISCs. The company also offers chemicals, such as phthalate plasticizers, phthalic anhydride, alcohols, glycols, curing agents, hydrogen peroxide, bisphenol-A, maleic anhydride, epoxidized soybean oil, stabilizers, liquefied gas, and epoxy resin, as well as 1,4-butanediol. In addition, it provides electronic materials comprising copper clad laminate, glass fabrics, liquid crystal display, touch panel, copper foil, and [[PCB]] ink; fiber and textile products, which consist of draw textured yarn, spin draw yarn, fully oriented yarn, partially oriented yarn, polyester staple fiber, PET resin, polyester film, polyester release film, woven fabric, and knitting fabric; and machinery and switchgear, including HV/LV switchgear cast resin transformer, mold, roller, and screw and barrel. Nan Ya Plastics Corporation was incorporated in 1958 and is headquartered in Kaohsiung, Taiwan.
