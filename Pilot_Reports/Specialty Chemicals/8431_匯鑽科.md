Superior Plating Technology Co., Ltd. operates as a surface treatment service provider and planting management company in Taiwan. The company offers surface treatment services for metal or plastic products, as well as related management consulting services, such as environmental, automation, and production services; and is involved in the wholesale of electrical appliances and industrial additives. It serves the HDD, electronics, automotive, thermal, precision instrument, and connector industries. The company was founded in 1997 and is headquartered in Taipei City, Taiwan.
