Forward Graphic Enterprise Co., Ltd. engages in the manufacture and sale of printed matter, plastic products, handicrafts, and cardboard and paper products in Taiwan. The company also offers box, gift box, chocolate box, and bag products; book; notebook; catalogue/flyer; calendar; and special, DIY, and eco friendly products. In addition, it provides product design, prepress, printing equipment, and post press services. The company was incorporated in 1968 and is based in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
