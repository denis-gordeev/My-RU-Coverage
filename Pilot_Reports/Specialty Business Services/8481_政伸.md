**產業:** Specialty Business Services
**市值:** 2,811 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 1,980 百萬台幣

Transart Graphics Co., Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, manufacture, process, sell, and import and export various labeled products for bicycles, bicycle parts, sports equipment, and other industries in Taiwan and internationally. The company offers water transfer decals, self-adhesive stickers, heat transfer decals, light pattern transfer, frost reflective decal, hologram irised decal, titanium flash, nameplates and graphic overlays, dry rub-on transfer, and brush metal-like nameplates. Transart Graphics Co., Ltd. was founded in 1976 and is based in Taichung, Taiwan.
