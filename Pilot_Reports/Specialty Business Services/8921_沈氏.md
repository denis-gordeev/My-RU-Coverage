## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Industrials
**產業:** Specialty Business Services
**市值:** 846 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 1,217 百萬台幣

Shen's Art Printing Co., Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, manufactures, processes, and sells various printed materials in Taiwan and internationally. The company offers creative design services, such as event execution and promotion campaign planning, editing, and visual portion; various services for small quantity orders and large format printing services; and special printing services, such as 3D lenticular and dynamic printing, as well as pattern technology. It also provides cultural and commercial printing for magazines, books, commercial promotion materials, stationary packs, and posters and pamphlet products; and delicate packages and handbags. The company was founded in 1977 and is based in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
