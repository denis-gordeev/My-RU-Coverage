Dacome International Ltd. distributes and sells golf products in Taiwan and internationally. The company offers golf equipment; woods; iron set; bags, such as clothing and ball bags; clothing, including long-sleeved crew neck shirts, trousers, short-sleeved polo and crew neck shirts, shorts, and socks; and footwear which includes spiked and spikeless shoes, as well as sporting goods and accessories. It also provides travel agency and other related services through electronic websites and direct stores. In addition, the company engages in the research, development, and sale of home rehabilitation fitness equipment. Dacome International Ltd. was founded in 1990 and is headquartered in Kaohsiung, Taiwan.
