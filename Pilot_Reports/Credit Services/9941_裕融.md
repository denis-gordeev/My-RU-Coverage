Yulon Finance Corporation provides various financial services in China, Taiwan, and internationally. The company offers vehicle and personal financing services, including new and used car, and auto mortgage loans; motorcycle loan installments; and auto leasing services, as well as installment plans for other products and services. It also offers corporate financing services, such as installment plans for heavy vehicles and equipment leasing services. In addition, the company offers used car sale intermediation services comprising auto auction and vehicle inspection. Yulon Finance Corporation was incorporated in 1990 and is based in Taipei, Taiwan.
