## 業務簡介

**產業:** Packaging & Containers
**市值:** 6,176 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 2,405 百萬台幣

Great China Metal Ind. Co., Ltd. manufactures and supplies food and beverage packaging containers in Taiwan and internationally. It produces and sells two-piece aluminum cans and cap wire products; porous films made of ethylene polymers; and metal two-piece cans. The company also provides printing and packaging services for various metal. In addition, it manufactures and trades metal containers, plastic products, and container packaging machine. The company was founded in 1973 and is based in Taipei, Taiwan.
