Taiwan Hon Chuan Enterprise Co., Ltd. manufactures and sells various packaging materials for the food and beverage industries in Taiwan, Mainland China, Southeast Asia, and internationally. The company offers aluminum, plastic, and metal caps; polyethylene terephthalate bottle/perform, and label products; and LDPE shrinkable films. It also provides beverage filling original equipment manufacturer services; and automatic sealer machines. The company was formerly known as Hon Chuan Metal Development Co., Ltd. and changed its name to Taiwan Hon Chuan Enterprise Co., Ltd. in 1999. Taiwan Hon Chuan Enterprise Co., Ltd. was incorporated in 1969 and is headquartered in Taichung, Taiwan.
