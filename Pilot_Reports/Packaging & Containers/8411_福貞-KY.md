Kingcan Holdings Limited, together with its subsidiaries, engages in the research, development, manufacture, and sale of various metal packaging containers and filling and canning in China and internationally. It also engages in trading; food cans to beverage and food manufacturers; beverage filling and bottling services; and canned filling [[OEM]] activities. Kingcan Holdings Limited was founded in 1993 and is based in Grand Cayman, the Cayman Islands.
