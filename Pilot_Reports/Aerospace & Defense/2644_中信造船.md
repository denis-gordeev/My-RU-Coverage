Jong Shyn Shipbuilding Co., Ltd. engages in ship building, repair, and maintenance businesses in Taiwan. The company manufactures freighters, frigates, tugboats, shuttle boats, fishing boats, ferries, yachts, oil tankers, research vessels, and land-based engineering equipment, as well as special ships. It also provides docking, mechanical maintenance, and coating services; shaft processing services; interior design and engineering services; as well as refrigeration and storage center services. Jong Shyn Shipbuilding Co., Ltd. was founded in 1973 and is based in Kaohsiung, Taiwan.
