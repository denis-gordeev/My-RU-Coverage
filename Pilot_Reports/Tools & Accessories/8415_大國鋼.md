It also engages in the import, export, and sale of aluminium and protective products; provision of energy technical services; production of pistons for motorcycles, commercial vehicles, and automobiles; investment activities; trading of automobiles engine parts; production of connecting rods; and manufacture of engine and automobiles parts. Brighton-Best International (Taiwan) Inc. was incorporated in 2007 and is headquartered in Tainan City, Taiwan.
