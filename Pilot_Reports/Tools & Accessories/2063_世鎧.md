## 業務簡介

Sheh Kai Precision Co., Ltd. manufactures and sells bi metal screws, screw anchors, and screws in Taiwan, the United Kingdom, the United States, Australia, Germany, and internationally. The company offers bi-metal window, concrete, self tapping, self drilling, and wing screws, as well as self piercing and side lap screws; hammer and diamond drill bits, and dust caps; and bi-metal concrete screw anchors, bi-metal concrete anchor stainless steel internal thread heads, galvanized concrete screw anchors, and galvanized concrete screw anchors internal thread heads. It also provides wood screws, stainless steel self-tapping screw sharps/blunt points, special fasteners, hex lag screws, double end hanger bolts, carriage bolts, and automotive fasteners. In addition, the company offers timber, decking, machine, coach, and special screws, as well as carriage bolts. Its products are used in various applications, such as roof wall, curtain wall window, and concrete anchor, as well as concrete anchor products. The company was formerly known as Sheh Kai Industrial Co., Ltd. and changed its name to Sheh Kai Precision Co., Ltd. in 2003. Sheh Kai Precision Co., Ltd. was founded in 1992 and is based in Kaohsiung, Taiwan.
