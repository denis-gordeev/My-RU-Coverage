# 8930 - 青鋼

## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Industrials
**產業:** Building Products & Equipment
**市值:** 2,509 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 2,517 百萬台幣

CKM Applied Materials Corp. engages in the manufacture and trading of building materials in Taiwan and internationally. The company offers ceiling suspension systems, such as T grids and partition frame systems; metal ceilings, including non-perforated, perforated, embossed, mesh, baffle, curve, linear, honeycomb, extrusion, crossing, triangular, cell, and strip ceilings; and micro-perforated acoustical panels. Its products are used in governmental buildings, hospitals, schools, department stores, hotels, factories, public buildings, and residential and commercial buildings. The company was formerly known as CKM Building Material Corp. and changed its name to CKM Applied Materials Corp. in July 2017. CKM Applied Materials Corp. was incorporated in 1982 and is headquartered in Tainan city, Taiwan.
