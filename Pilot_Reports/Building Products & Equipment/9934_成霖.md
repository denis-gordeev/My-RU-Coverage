## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Industrials
**產業:** Building Products & Equipment
**市值:** 4,094 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 7,119 百萬台幣

Globe Union Industrial Corp., through its subsidiaries, engages in the manufacture and sale of plumbing products in the United States, Mainland China, the United Kingdom, Canada, and internationally. It offers faucets and related parts; showerheads and vitreous products; porcelain bathroom fittings; kitchen and bathroom products and accessories; and fixtures and fittings, valves, and other plumbing related products. The company also provides marketing and sales support services, as well as operates a sales and maintenance center; as a trust company; and in the consulting industry. In addition, it invests in, develops, and manufactures hardware products. The company markets its products under the Gerber, Prima, Lenz, Prima, and Bathrooms To Love brands. It serves as an [[OEM]] source of products. The company was incorporated in 1979 and is headquartered in Taichung, Taiwan.
