PADAUK Technology Company Limited engages in the research, development, design, manufacture, and sale of integrated circuit products. Its products are used in interactive smart toys, remote control and smart household appliances, industrial grade chips, and high-efficiency brushless DC motors. The company is also involved in the manufacturing of electrical components; retail sale of electronic materials; and international trade, as well as provision of technical services. PADAUK Technology Company Limited was founded in 2005 and is based in Hsinchu City, Taiwan.
