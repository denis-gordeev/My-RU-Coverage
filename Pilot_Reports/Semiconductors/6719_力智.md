## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Semiconductors
**市值:** 19,406 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 8,103 百萬台幣

uPI Semiconductor Corp., together with its subsidiaries, primarily engages in design, research, development, and sales of various integrated circuits in Taiwan and internationally. The company offers power management products, such as PMICs, DC-DC Controllers and converters, power device drivers, low dropout regulators, power switch ICs, power monitoring and current consoles, charging ICs, and integrated power stage products; battery management products, including PTIC and charger ICs; low and middle voltage power MOSFET products; OP-amplifiers; and [[GaN]] solutions comprising [[GaN]] information and FET Driver products, as well as uGANs. The company was founded in 2005 and is headquartered in Zhubei, Taiwan.
