# 3712 - 永崴投控

## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Semiconductors
**市值:** 6,094 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 50,735 百萬台幣

FIT Holding Co., Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, engages in production, manufacturing, and trading of optical instrument components. It offers computer peripheral components, 3C products, image scanners, multifunction printers, and investment and development of power plant and cleaning energy services. The company also Involved in manufacturing and selling of optical lens components, image scanners and multifunction printers, telecommunication components, components of communication and consumer electronics, and parts and moulds of photocopiers and scanners. In addition, it is involved in energy service management; general investments holding; sales agency; production and processing and sale of optical lens components and other products; energy service management; mould development and moulding tool manufacturing; sale of electronic telecommunication components; specialized investments; sale of medical instruments; supply chain finance energy service management; natural gas service management; afforestation; electricity generating; maritime engineering; and energy technical services. The company was incorporated in 2018 and is based in New Taipei City, Taiwan.

## 供應鏈位置
永崴投控透過旗下子公司佈局電子與能源產業鏈：
- **電子產業 (中下游)**：
    - **上游**：光燿科技從事光學鏡頭與[[光通訊]]元件製造。
    - **中游**：崴強科技專注於影像感測模組與掃描器製造；勁永國際提供專業 [[EMS]] 代工服務及[[記憶體]]模組。
    - **下游**：透過集團通路銷售 [[Apple]] 周邊配件及自有品牌產品。
- **能源產業 (中下游)**：
    - **中游**：森崴能源從事電廠開發、工程統包 (EPC)。
    - **下游**：提供綠電銷售及電廠維運 (O&M) 服務。

## 主要客戶及供應商
- **主要客戶**：
    - **電子產品**：[[Apple]] (透過集團關係密切合作)、國際印表機大廠 (如 [[HP]], Epson 等由崴強代工)、以及各式消費電子品牌。
    - **能源服務**：台電 (離岸風電與太陽能案場)、台灣[[半導體]]大廠 (綠電採購)。
- **主要供應商**：
    - **電子零組件**：IC 設計廠、被動元件廠、塑膠機構件供應商。
    - **能源設備**：太陽能模組廠、風力發電機組供應商、儲能電池供應商。
