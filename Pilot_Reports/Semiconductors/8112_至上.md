## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Semiconductors

**企業價值:** 65,668 百萬台幣

Supreme Electronics Co., Ltd. engages in the import and export dealership of electronic products and components in Taiwan, Hong Kong, China, the United States, and internationally. The company offers memory products and solutions, such as [[DRAM]], flash, and mobile solutions; system LSI products and solutions; mobile and display LED's; multilayer ceramic capacitors; polarizers and batteries; cold plate and direct liquid cooling, and immersion cooling products; and encoders, TV decoders, DVI encoders, and LVDS encoders. It also develops high-precision analog and mixed-signal integrated circuits for the audio and energy markets; USB audio business; car displays, TFT-LCD for the notebook computer monitors, desktop computer monitors, as well as network information appliances, audiovisual equipment, and automotive products; energy-related material solutions; and solutions for cloud computing and data center markets. In addition, the company provides LED driver ICs; digital RF transmission for video and audio products; timing controller IC products; and is involved in the digital transformation of energy efficiency management and automation in homes, commercial buildings, data centers, infrastructure, and industry. Supreme Electronics Co., Ltd. was incorporated in 1987 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
