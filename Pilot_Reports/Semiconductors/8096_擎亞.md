## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology

**企業價值:** 12,806 百萬台幣

CoAsia Electronics Corp. provides components for mobile multimedia in Taiwan, China, the United States, Southeast Asia, India, and Korea. The company offers CMOS, 3D, and automotive image sensors; processors, PMICs, and NFC components; memory products, such as [[DRAM]], SSD, eStorage, and MCP; and foundry solutions. It also provides mobile and IT AMOLEDs; OIS+AF controller and driver ICs for camera modules, and MicroLED driver IC, as well as micro controller units for remote controllers, small home application, and LCD applications. The company was formerly known as CoAsia Microelectronics Corp. CoAsia Electronics Corp. was founded in 1997 and is based in Taipei, Taiwan.
