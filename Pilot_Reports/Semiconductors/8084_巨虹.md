Chip Hope Co., Ltd, together with its subsidiaries, engages in the manufacture and trading of memory products in Taiwan and China. The company offers memory products for memory cards, medical equipment products, and acoustic devices. It also involved in the trading of electronic component materials, semiconductors, and communication devices and software; development of electronic products and new electronic components and devices; manufacturing and wholesaling of machinery, other machinery, and equipment; restaurant business; and food wholesale business. The company was founded in 1982 and is based in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
