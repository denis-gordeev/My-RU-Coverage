## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Semiconductors
**市值:** 5,398 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 4,423 百萬台幣

Progate Group Corporation engages in the design, development, manufactures, sale, and trading of integrated circuits in Taiwan. It is also involved in intellectual property rights; wholesale of electronic materials and information software; and agency and import and export trade of various related products. The company also offers ASIC design service; ASIC mass production service; IP turnkey service; APR layout turnkey service; gate array turnkey service; COT/foundry turnkey service; cyberShuttle/MPW/prototype service; [[TSMC]] CoWoS turnkey service; and [[TSMC]] InFO services. Progate Group Corporation was founded in 1980 and is based in Taipei, Taiwan.
