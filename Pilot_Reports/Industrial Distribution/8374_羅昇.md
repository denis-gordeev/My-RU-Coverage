## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Industrials
**產業:** Industrial Distribution
**市值:** 9,496 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 10,397 百萬台幣

Ace Pillar Co., Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, engages in the testing, processing, selling, repairment, electromechanical integration of automation control and mechanical transmission system; and intelligent technology service. It offers motion control and monitor systems [[IPC]], motion controller, CNC controller, PLC, HMI, ethernet solution, remote IO, static eliminators, power supply, sensor and high precision measurement. In addition, the company provides VFD and smart energy, VFD for elevator, PMSM, servo motor, DD motor, linear module, and precision positioning; mechanic transmission, which include reducers, coupling, power locks, electromagnetic clutch, safety brake, industrial LED, ball screw/ linear guide, and ball bearing/freebear; and pneumatic fluid components consisting of FRL combination, solenoid valve, fluid valve, cylinder, liquid valve, fittings, vacuum pump, hydraulic, flow switch, reed switch, and others. Further, it offers robotics and machine vision; stapping machines and other equipment; charging pile and energy; and software products; e-commerce business; and automation control and industrial transmission systems. The company was incorporated in 1984 and is headquartered in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
