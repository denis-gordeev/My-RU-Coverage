## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Consumer Cyclical
**產業:** Auto Manufacturers
**市值:** 47,323 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 86,920 百萬台幣

Sanyang Motor Co., Ltd. engages in the manufacture and sale of automobiles, motorcycles, and their components in Taiwan, China, Asia, Europe, and internationally. The company is also involved in the provision of distribution, repair, and maintenance of automobiles and its parts; product design; rental and leasing of passenger cars; waste disposal; manufacturing, research, development and sale of aluminum battery-related and renewable energy products; sale of electricity; property insurance agency business; manufacturing, processing, and trading of hardware machinery and iron material; real estate development and management; provides elderly housing; sale of camper and camper sales, provides leisure events and services, and other entertainment services; and investment activities. In addition, it provides related technical and consulting services. The company offers its products under the SYM and HYUNDAI brands. The company was formerly known as Sanyang Industry Co., Ltd. and changed its name to Sanyang Motor Co., Ltd. in January 2015. Sanyang Motor Co., Ltd. was founded in 1954 and is headquartered in Hsinchu City, Taiwan.
