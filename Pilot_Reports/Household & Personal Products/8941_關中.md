Grand Hall Enterprise Co., Ltd. designs, produces, and sells outdoor gas supplies in Taiwan, North America, Europe, and Australia. The company offers barbecue grills and outdoor living products. It also provides LP gas cylinder valves, brass forged parts, gas valves, and scuba diving valves; and large-scale gas ovens, heaters, and accessories, as well as barbecue stoves, water heaters, and outdoor decorative flames. The company was founded in 1965 and is based in Taipei, Taiwan.
