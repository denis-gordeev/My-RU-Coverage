**板塊:** Consumer Defensive

**企業價值:** 425 百萬台幣

Fu Burg Industrial Co., Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, manufactures and sells adult diapers in Taiwan. The company offers adult care products, including incontinence brief pants, pads, and personal cleansing wipes. It also provides baby care products, such as diapers, pants, and water wipes. In addition, the company offers female care products comprising menstrual pants, napkin, and tampon under the ANAN brand; incontinence lite pads; and wet wipes for women. Further, it provides face masks for skin care. It offers its products under the Feelfree, Tino, Medicos, Mori, ANAN, and Ancare brand names. Fu Burg Industrial Co., Ltd. was founded in 1977 and is based in Taipei, Taiwan.
