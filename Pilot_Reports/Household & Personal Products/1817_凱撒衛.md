Sanitar Co., Ltd. engages in the manufacture and distribution of sanitary porcelain products in Taiwan and internationally. It offers bathroom fixtures, including bathtubs, toilets, machines, lavatories, cabinets, mirrors, hand showers, and copper faucets. The company was formerly known as Sanken Industrial Co., Ltd. and changed its name to Sanitar Co., Ltd. in 2003. Sanitar Co., Ltd. was founded in 1985 and is based in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
