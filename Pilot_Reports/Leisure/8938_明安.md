Advanced International Multitech Co., Ltd. engages in the manufacture and sale of golf equipment, golf ball, composites, and bicycle parts in the Americas, Asia, and internationally. It offers golf products, including metal woods, irons/wedges, putters, graphite shafts, and club assemblies; carbon fiber bicycle components, such as frames, forks, and parts; thermoset and thermoplastic composites; investment casting services for components of aircraft interior, bike, industrial, and hardware in titanium and stainless steel; and CFRP wheels. The company is also involved in production of plastic products, as well as engages in the international trade, and import and export of carbon fiber prepreg materials and sports products. Advanced International Multitech Co., Ltd. was incorporated in 1987 and is headquartered in Kaohsiung, Taiwan.
