Bonny Worldwide Limited, together with its subsidiaries, engages in the manufacture and sale of [[OEM]] and [[ODM]] carbon fiber rackets and related sporting goods in China. The company offers hockey sticks, tennis and badminton rackets, and carbon fiber rims. It also provides carbon fiber sports products, such as hockey sticks, helmets, and baseball bats. In addition, the company operates and manages a sports center. The company offers its products under the BONNY brand name. Bonny Worldwide Limited was founded in 1982 and is headquartered in Grand Cayman, the Cayman Islands.
