WW Holding Inc., together with its subsidiaries, engages in the manufacturing and sale of sports equipment, clothing, accessories, handbags, belts, suitcases, and leather accessories. It operates in two segments, Sports Equipment Business and Luxury Bag Business. The company offers sports and medical gears, such as calf pressure sleeves, functional sports socks, greaves, soft helmets, and sports gloves; sports and golf bags; luggages; and luxury bags, including casual and tote bags, and wallets. It is also involved in the trading and land development businesses. The company operates in the United States, Mainland China, Belgium, France, Germany, and internationally. WW Holding Inc. was founded in 1998 and is headquartered in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
