**企業價值:** 58,663 百萬台幣

Ruentex Engineering & Construction Co., Ltd. engages in the construction and civil engineering contracting business in Taiwan. It operates through the Construction Business, Building Materials, and Interior Decoration Design segments. The company engages in electromechanical installation engineering; water installation, refrigeration, and air conditioning engineering; planning, design and consultancy for civil engineering and precast engineering; import and export trade, distribution, and agency of the building materials and equipment; and design and construction of interior decoration and landscaping design. It also produces and sells precast beams, columns, slabs, exterior walls, and various precast building structural components for civil engineering; mud wall primer and finishing materials, tile adhesives, self-leveling cement, and dry-mixed cement mortar; and cement and semi-finished cement products. Ruentex Resources Integration Co., Ltd. was founded in 1975 and is based in Taipei, Taiwan.
