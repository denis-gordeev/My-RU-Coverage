# 1594 - 日高

## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Industrials
**產業:** Engineering & Construction
**市值:** 281 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 181 百萬台幣

Ju-Kao Engineering Co., Ltd. engages in the steel frame and steel structure ironwork maintenance projects, heavy machinery cold work maintenance projects, and mechanical processing projects. The company provides engineering solutions, including design, constructions, maintenance, safety precautions, and construction management; designs, constructs, and produces factory buildings, steel frame structures, and mechanical equipment; procuring materials and equipment, and design and installation of plant equipment services; manufacturing and installation of dust collection equipment; as well as on-site maintenance services. It also offers lightweight solar power panel design solutions; solar panel installation; customized design and evaluation; system performance evaluation; smart monitoring and energy management systems; and emergency maintenance services. In addition, the company provides mobile solar cold storage, commercial solar freezer, smart temperature control system, smart warehouse system, solar refrigerated factory, and factory energy saving retrofit. Further, it operates in SRC steel structure construction, floating dock, harbor, and industrial plant equipment integration turnkey projects; conveying, soil and air pollution control, noise control, and water and sewage treatment equipment, as well as lifting and crane equipment; fabricates and pipes oil tanks, tower tanks, storage tanks, and pressure vessels; and undertakes metal painting and coating projects, surveying, drilling, and earthworks; disposal of sand, gravel, and silt sea. Additionally, the company engages in the landscape engineering; installation of cable and elevator; computer equipment, satellite TV, window and glass, and kitchenware and bathroom equipment installation businesses; traffic sign; drilling engineering; static electricity protection and elimination; and nuclear engineering. Ju-Kao Engineering Co., Ltd. was founded in 1963 and is headquartered in Kaohsiung City, Taiwan.

## 供應鏈位置
日高位於工程建設及設備維護產業鏈中下游。
*   **上游:** 採購鋼材、管材、機械設備零組件。
*   **中游:** 工程設計、鋼構製作、設備安裝、維修保養。
*   **下游:** 服務於鋼鐵廠、石化廠、電廠等大型工業客戶。
