## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Consumer Cyclical
**產業:** Textile Manufacturing
**市值:** 15,554 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 27,126 百萬台幣

Taiwan Paiho Limited manufactures and sells touch fasteners, webbing, elastic, easy tape, jacquard digital woven fabric, jacquard engineered mesh, and other functional auxiliary materials and textile fabrics in Taiwan, China, Vietnam, and internationally. The company offers webbing, such as footwear, clothes, bag webbing, shoelaces and draw cords, etc.; and pull strings with various tips, reflective materials, and jacquard fabric weaving functions. It also provides Jacquard engineered mesh for shoe uppers, apparel, home accessories bags, baby strollers, and houseware; touch fastener for accessories of shoes, garments, raincoats, caps, sports equipment, leather goods, and medical equipment; various knitted elastic, flat woven elastic, jacquard elastic, and multi directional textile with elasticity; and easy tape products for paper diapers, medical equipment, computer pipelines, outdoor sporting goods, car seats and interiors, aircraft seats and interiors, and other accessories. In addition, the company offers finishing services for bamboo charcoal yarn; Jacquard digital woven fabric used as external fabrics for footwear, boutique bags and apparels; and thermal insulation products. The company was formerly known as San Ho Hsin Limited and changed its name to Taiwan Paiho Limited in 1986. Taiwan Paiho Limited was founded in 1979 and is headquartered in Changhua, Taiwan.
