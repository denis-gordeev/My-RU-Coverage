# 6508 - 惠光

## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Basic Materials
**產業:** Agricultural Inputs
**市值:** 2,038 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 1,452 百萬台幣

Huikwang Corporation engages in the manufacture, processing, packaging, and trading of various pesticides, fertilizers, and public health pesticides. It is also involved in the manufacture of plastic sheets, fabrics, pipes and tubes, plastic films and bags, industrial plastic products, and geosynthetics, as well as micronutrient and organic fertilizer, and glyphosate, and other coating materials; plant growth enhancers and other products; and import and export trade. The company operates in Taiwan, Mainland China, Brazil, Vietnam, Thailand, Indonesia, the Philippines, Australia, the United States, and internationally. Huikwang Corporation was founded in 1954 and is based in Tainan City, Taiwan.
