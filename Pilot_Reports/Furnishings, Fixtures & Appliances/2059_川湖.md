King Slide Works Co., Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, designs, manufactures, and sells rail kits for computer and network communications equipment, furniture wooden kitchen accessories, slides, and molds in Taiwan, the United States, China, and internationally. The company offers ultra-thin server slide rail kits, electronic device rail kits, and cable management arm for cloud computing data centers, as well as engages in the international investment activities. It also provides kitchen/furniture solutions, including metal and wood drawer systems, inner drawers and pull out systems, undermount slides, ball bearing slides, epoxy coating drawer slides, and hinges for home furniture, kitchen cabinets and work space; and consumer electronics services. In addition, the company is involved in trading and wholesale of rail kits, slides, and accessories; sale of communication equipment and related products; import and export of goods and technologies; and manufacture and sale of mechanical rail kits, servers, handheld wireless phones, electronic products, internet-related products, peripheral components, and data centers with rail kits. Its products are used in computer and network communications equipment, furniture wooden kitchen accessories, slides and molds, home appliances, motorhomes and RVs, office equipment, medical storage, and other applications. King Slide Works Co., Ltd. was incorporated in 1986 and is headquartered in Kaohsiung, Taiwan.
