Redwood Group Ltd, together with its subsidiaries, engages in the decoration of boutique brand stores in Asia, the Middle East, the United States, and Europe. It also operates as a contract manufacturer of interior fittings for luxury brands; and designs, produces, and sells millwork, metal and PVD, glass, veneer, LED and electrical, paint coating, acrylic, sofa and upholstery, solid surface and stone processing, and packing. The company was founded in 1992 and is based in Taipei City, Taiwan.
