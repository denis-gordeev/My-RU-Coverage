Ching Feng Home Fashions Co.,Ltd engages in the manufacture and sale of home decor products in Taiwan. The company provides folding curved windows, folding curtains, black curtains, and heavy-duty parts. It also offers aluminum venetian, faux wood venetian, vinyl venetian, and vertical blinds; roman, pleated, and cellular shades; and coral fleece throws and blankets, flannel throws and blankets, faux fur throws, décor throws, and comforters. The company also offers decor pillows; and bath products, such as shower curtains. In addition, it provides beddings, table linens, bath accessories, pillows, cushions, and kitchen appliances. Ching Feng Home Fashions Co.,Ltd was founded in 1974 and is based in Changhua, Taiwan.
