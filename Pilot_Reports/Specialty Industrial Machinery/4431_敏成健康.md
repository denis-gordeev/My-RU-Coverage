## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Industrials
**產業:** Specialty Industrial Machinery
**市值:** 680 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 610 百萬台幣

Mytrex Health Technologies, Inc. engages in the manufacturing, processing, and sale of various filter non-woven fabrics in Taiwan, rest of Asia, Europe, and internationally. The company provides electrostatic filtration materials for medical and industrial mask media, and other filtration applications; and composite nonwoven products for coolant and air filter, vacuum filter bag, HEPA and cabin filter, and water filtration compound material applications. It also offers thermal insulation products for glove, sock, shoe, hat, clothing material, and mask insulation material applications; and linen laundry and leasing, logistic, and medical supplies for hospitals. In addition, the company sells pharmaceuticals and medical hygiene materials; and manages medical clothing products. Mytrex Health Technologies, Inc. was founded in 1990 and is based in Taoyuan City, Taiwan.
