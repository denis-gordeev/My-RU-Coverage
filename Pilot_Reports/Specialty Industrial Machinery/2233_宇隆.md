TURVO International Co., Ltd. engages in the development, production, and sale of precision metal parts and components. Its products are used in connector, sensor, industrial automation, medical equipment components, temperature control equipment, bicycle components, automotive engine, steering, drivetrain, chassis, and security systems. TURVO International Co., Ltd. operates in China, Germany, Turkey, Taiwan, Mexico, India, Hungary, Poland, Japan, the United States, and internationally. The company was incorporated in 1987 and is based in Taichung City, Taiwan.
