## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Industrials
**產業:** Specialty Industrial Machinery
**市值:** 6,908 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 8,333 百萬台幣

Patec Precision Industry Co., Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, provides components manufacturing and engineering solutions to the metal-forming industry in Singapore, Indonesia, China, Thailand, Vietnam, and Europe. The company offers [[ODM]] precision mechanical press machines and tool design and fabrication, as well as robotics transfer, feeder, and other ancillary support systems. It also develops, fabricates, and tests precision tools for custom applications and process conversions. In addition, the company supplies precision metal formed components and sub-assembled components to automotive and electronics companies. Further, it is involved in investment holding; and the production and sale of stamping machines, automobile and motorcycle parts, service robots, and HDD hard drive gripper arm related products. It serves engine transmission systems, exhaust systems, brake and safety systems, consumer electronic products, mobile devices, and hard disk drives. Patec Precision Industry Co., Ltd. was founded in 1992 and is based in Singapore.
