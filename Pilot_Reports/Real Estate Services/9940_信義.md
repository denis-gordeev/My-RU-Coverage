Sinyi Realty Inc., together with its subsidiaries, operates in the real estate brokerage and development businesses in Taiwan, Mainland China, and internationally. The company offers transactions and leasing of residential, office, industrial, and land properties. It engages in residential development and sales, as well as invest in Sabah Malaysia's tourism sector, promoting eco-tourism resorts. In addition, the company provides property management, consulting, IT and data services, and construction management The company was incorporated in 1987 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
