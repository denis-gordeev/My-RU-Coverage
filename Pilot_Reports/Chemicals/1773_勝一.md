## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Basic Materials
**產業:** Chemicals
**市值:** 43,800 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 46,745 百萬台幣

Shiny Chemical Industrial Co., Ltd. engages in manufacturing, processing, and trading of chemical solvents in Taiwan and internationally. It provides electronic solvents, including menthyl acetates, ether alcohol esters, other solvents, and customized products; and industrial solvents, such as menthyl acetates, ether ester products, and other solvents. The company also offers formalin and urea glue products; and leases storage tanks and warehouses. In addition, it provides electronic grade solvents for use in semiconductor process, flat screen monitor, photoresist manufacturing, bumping and packaging, and LED industries. Shiny Chemical Industrial Co., Ltd. was incorporated in 1979 and is headquartered in Kaohsiung, Taiwan.
