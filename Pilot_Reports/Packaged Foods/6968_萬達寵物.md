Wonder Pets Enterprises Corporation, together with its subsidiaries, wholesales and retails pet food and supplies in Taiwan. The company offers pet dating, mating, grooming, accommodation, and after school care services. It operates under the Pet Park, Hair Market, Kaido Pet, and Cat and Dog Captain brands. The company also sells its products and services through online and offline stores. Wonder Pets Enterprises Corporation was founded in 2011 and is based in Taipei, Taiwan.
