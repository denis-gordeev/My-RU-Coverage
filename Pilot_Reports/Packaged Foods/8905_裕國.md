# 8905 - 裕國

## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Consumer Defensive
**產業:** Packaged Foods
**市值:** 4,538 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 4,966 百萬台幣

Eagle Cold Storage Enterprise Co., Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, engages in the processing and trading of vegetables and frozen meat products in Taiwan. It operates through three segments: Warehousing Department, Meats Segment, and Construction Segment. The company leases refrigeration equipment and cold storage solutions; and warehouse for vegetables, fruit, fish, meat, and ice products. It also manufactures and processes agricultural goods and food; sells and procures meat and vegetable products; and researches and develops new products. In addition, the company develops, leases, and sells commercial properties and lands, as well as constructs residential properties. Eagle Cold Storage Enterprise Co., Ltd. was founded in 1979 and is headquartered in Taichung, Taiwan.
