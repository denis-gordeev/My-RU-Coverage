**板塊:** Industrials
**產業:** Waste Management

**企業價值:** 4,555 百萬台幣

Taiwan Environment Scientific Co., Ltd. engages in environmental protection, soil and groundwater investigation and remediation projects, and construction waste treatment businesses. The company operates through three segments: Project, Land Resource, and other segments. It engages in the management of land resources and waste disposal; consultation, remediation and investigation. In addition, the company engages in the biological reproduction of contaminated soil; investment holding; technical services; and disposal of incinerator bottom ash. Taiwan Environment Scientific Co., Ltd. was founded in 2003 and is headquartered in Kaohsiung, Taiwan.
