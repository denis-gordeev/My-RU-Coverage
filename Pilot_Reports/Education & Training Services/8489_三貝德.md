**企業價值:** 1,463 百萬台幣

Samebest Co., LTD. engages in the research and development of digital cloud course systems primarily in Taiwan. It operates through Digital Information Business, Business Services, and Supplementary Education Business segments. The company also develops personalized action learning solutions through the integration of virtual and real. Its products cover high school, middle school, elementary school middle and upper grades, elementary school lower middle grade, and preschool. The company was founded in 2006 and is based in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
