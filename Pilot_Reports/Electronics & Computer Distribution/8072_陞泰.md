## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Electronics & Computer Distribution
**市值:** 2,392 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 1,379 百萬台幣

AV TECH Corporation manufactures and sells surveillance products in Taiwan. The company offers network recorders, IP cameras and recorder, HD CCTV recorders and cameras, and peripherals, as well as PoE switches products. It also provides central management system software for PCs; EagleEyes software; IP scan tool; and VWS Remote Desktop that allows simultaneous log in to recorders for remote monitoring. The company's products are used in various industries that includes chain stores, hotels, banks, apartment buildings/complexes, Wi-Fi surveillance, hospital and health institutions, manufacturing plants, shopping malls, museums, agriculture, construction sites, fitness centers and gyms, airports, train stations and metro, cloud storage, casinos, cyber defender, education, and private security. AV TECH Corporation was founded in 1996 and is based in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
