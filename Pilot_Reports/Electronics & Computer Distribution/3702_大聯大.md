## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Electronics & Computer Distribution
**市值:** 108,299 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 259,836 百萬台幣

WPG Holdings Limited distributes and sells electronic and electrical components, computer software, and electrical products in Taiwan, Mainland China, and internationally. It offers core components, including [[AI]] accelerator, chipset, graphics/audio/video controller, smartphone chip, network/modern chipset, self-driving control chip, [[MCU]], GPU, CPU/MPU, and RISC CPU; analog IC and mixed-signal IC comprising bipolar, CMOS operational amplifier, comparator, digital/analog converter, power supply controller, and audio and video amplifier or controller; and discrete logic IC, such as diode, rectifier, transistor, thyristor, insulated-gate bipolar transistor (IGBT), [[SiC]], [[GaN]], optical transistor, and logic IC. The company also provides memory components, including [[DRAM]], MRAM, SRAM, EPROM, EEPROM, Flash, and MPC; optical components and sensors comprising LCD panel module, panel driver IC, panel touch IC, CMOS sensor, inertial sensor, LED, and Micro LED; and other non-electric components. In addition, it offers passive components, electromagnetic components, and connectors, such as resistor, capacitor, inductor, magnetic component, cable, connector, mechanics, electromechanics, circuit protection, material, battery, filter, oscillator, touchpad, and polarizer. Further, the company provides value-added services, including procurement, built-to-order, integrated solution, on-line, virtual managed inventory system, and financial support services, as well as warehouse, logistic, and cross-border support services. Additionally, it engages in warehousing services; investment activities; trading, import, and export of electronic components; sales of office machinery and equipment; and manufacturing of computer and peripherals. WPG Holdings Limited was founded in 1980 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
