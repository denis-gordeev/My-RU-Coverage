Trigold Holdings Limited, through its subsidiaries, engages in the agency and distribution of computer peripheral equipment and electronic components in Taiwan and China. The company engages in the B2C and O2O businesses; and operates indoor playground for kids. The company was founded in 1988 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan. Trigold Holdings Limited is a subsidiary of WPG Holdings Limited.
