**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Computer Hardware
**市值:** 122,186 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 116,868 百萬台幣

Chenbro Micom Co., Ltd. engages in the research and development, design, manufacture, processing, and trading of computer peripherals and system of expendables in the United States, China, Taiwan, Europe, and internationally. It provides barebone servers; and rackmount, tower server, and PC chassis products. The company offers storage expansion kits; and [[OEM]], [[ODM]], JDM services. In addition, it is involved in the software design activities; and export and import of computer products and peripherals. The company was incorporated in 1983 and is headquartered in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
