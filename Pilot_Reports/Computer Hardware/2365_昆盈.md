## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Computer Hardware
**市值:** 7,809 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 6,724 百萬台幣

KYE Systems Corp. engages in the manufacturing, processing, and sale of computer peripheral products for business, lifestyle, mobility, and gaming peripherals worldwide. The company provides mice, keyboards, speakers, headsets, webcams, graphic tablets, video imaging products, such as network and security cameras, headphone speakers, game console peripherals and other consumer electronics products, as well as racing wheels and game controllers. It also offers various accessories, such as hubs, fast chargers, cables, adaptors, mouse pads, presenter, tripod, stand, and others. In addition, the company provides [[AI]] Copilot peripherals. KYE Systems Corp. was founded in 1983 and is based in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
