**企業價值:** 675 百萬台幣

Enermax Technology Corporation manufactures, maintenance, and trades computer hardware and peripheral products in Taiwan, Europe, Asia, and the United States. The company offers power supplies; cases comprising full, mid, and mini towers; fans; air and liquid CPU coolers; peripherals, such as monitor stands, USB fans, air guides, headset holders, heatsinks, and laptop stands; and audio products, including speakers and earphones. It also trades in bicycles and its peripheral products. Enermax Technology Corporation was founded in 1980 and is headquartered in Taoyuan City, Taiwan.
