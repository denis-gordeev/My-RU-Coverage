## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Computer Hardware
**市值:** 6,927 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 10,407 百萬台幣

DFI Inc. engages in the manufacturing and sale of board cards and computer components for industrial computers in Asia, America, Europe, and Internationally. It operates through Board Cards And System and Industrial Automation Intelligence segments. The company also offers services for intelligent products of industrial automations. In addition, it offers industrial panel pcs and displays; system-on modules; peripherals; industrial computers; application-specific system; edge server; and software and services. Further, The company provides industry automation, medical, agile gaming, transportation, energy, rugged, [[5G]] and networking, edge [[AI]] platforms, out-of-band, and [[OEM]] solutions. It serves automation, medical, gaming, transportation, energy, mission-critical, and intelligent retail industries. DFI Inc. was incorporated in 1981 and is based in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
