Dexin Corporation engages in the design, development, engineering, manufacture, and sale of entertainment interface devices in Taiwan and internationally. The company offers esports peripherals, such as e-sports mouse, controllers, keyboards, and gaming mouse pad, as well as stylus; and optical products comprising camera modules, vehicle camera modules and media cameras. It also is involved in the provision of industrial design, research design development, product and manufacture engineering design development, and other customized project design and development services for original equipment and design manufacturers. The company was founded in 1997 and is headquartered in New Taipei City, Taiwan..
