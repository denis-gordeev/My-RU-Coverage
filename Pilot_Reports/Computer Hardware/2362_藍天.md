## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Computer Hardware
**市值:** 22,542 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 51,944 百萬台幣

Clevo Co., together with its subsidiaries, primarily engages in the design, manufacture, and sale of visual display units, computers, and peripheral devices in Taiwan, China, the Asia-Pacific, Europe, and the Americas. The company operates through Computer and Buynow Plaza segments. It is involved in the research, development, design, manufacture, retail, wholesale, maintenance, and after-sales service of computers, notebooks, tablets, information and communication products, computer components, computer software and hardware, and electronic digital technology products; provision of property and shopping mall management, building leasing, real estate advisory, housekeeping, parking lot, and car wash activities. The company also engages in the wholesale and retail, import and export, and aftersales service of household appliances, goods, communication equipment, electrical devices, office supplies, and complementary products; and development, technology transfer, advisory, and training services for internet, computer software and hardware, and communication equipment; and provision of market management services for operators of laptops, computers, tablets, desktops, palmtop computers, information and communication products, computer component, and electronic products. In addition, it is involved in the advisory of economic information, and business and investment management; wholesale agency of electronic products; leasing of buildings and facilities, as well as display venues of computer and related electronic products; and provision of catering management, business affairs, and information consultation services. It offers its products for gaming, entertainment, and mobility applications. The company was incorporated in 1983 and is headquartered in New Taipei City, Taiwan. Clevo Co. operates as a subsidiary of Taipei Twin Corporation.
