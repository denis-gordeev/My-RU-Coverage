## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Computer Hardware
**市值:** 12,746 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 13,216 百萬台幣

Alcor Micro,Corp. designs, develops, and markets integrated semiconductors and software solutions for the personal computer peripherals and consumer electronics in China, Taiwan, South Korea, and internationally. It offers application-specific integrated circuit (ASIC) services, including front-end design, IP design, physical implementation, wafer foundry solutions, advanced packaging solutions, product and reliability engineering, and product management services, as well as [[AI]] design and wireless platform solutions. The company's solutions are used in automotive, [[AI]]/machine learning, industrial [[IoT]], healthcare, telecommunication, and consumer electronics applications. The company was founded in 1999 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
