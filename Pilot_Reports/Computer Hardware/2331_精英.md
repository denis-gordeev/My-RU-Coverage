## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Computer Hardware
**市值:** 14,130 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 6,384 百萬台幣

Elitegroup Computer Systems Co.,Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, designs, develops, manufactures, and sells computer equipment in Asia, America, Europe, and internationally. The company offers motherboards, including [[Intel]] and [[AMD]] platforms, and SoC solutions; desktop computers; notebooks; tablet computers; barebone systems; and graphics cards. It also provides mini PCs under the LIVA brand; commercial and education laptops; chromebooks; AIO and gaming PCs; [[IPC]] motherboards; CubeSat; and wearables/[[IoT]], such as portable sharing AP, gaming headsets, [[EV]] AC chargers, and smart gateways. In addition, the company is involved in the manufacture and sale of computer peripheral products and related components, cases, and systems assembled; manufacture and maintenance of electrical equipment and instruments; manufacture of PCBs; trading in ICs and electric components; wholesale, trade, maintenance, and technical consultation for computers and peripheral products; provision of technical and sales support services; and sale of software. Elitegroup Computer Systems Co.,Ltd. was incorporated in 1987 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
