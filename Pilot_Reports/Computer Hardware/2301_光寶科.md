## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Computer Hardware
**市值:** 407,424 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 356,965 百萬台幣

Lite-On Technology Corporation, together with its subsidiaries, operates as an electronics company worldwide. The company offers optoelectronic semiconductors, such as visible UV and automotive LEDs, couplers, optical sensors, and IR emitters and detectors; energy management products, including data center power supply systems, cloud server chassis products, power supply modules, and magnetic components; and consumer electronics comprising 3C accessories and power supply modules. It also provides networking and surveillance products consisting of video surveillance products, access control and alarm products, networking solutions, and server and motherboard products; automotive electronics, such as [[ADAS]] solutions and AC and DC charging solutions; and roadway intellimation systems, including roadway lighting intellimation products, intelligent traffic signal systems, and [[AI]] predictive maintenance solutions. The company was founded in 1975 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
