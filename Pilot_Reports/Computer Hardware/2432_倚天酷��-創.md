Acer Gadget Inc. engages in the design, sales, and lease of computer software and hardware application systems in Taiwan and internationally. The company offers computer peripherals, such as Acer USB hub, keyboards, and mouse, as well as webcams, headsets, and speakerphones; epay wearable products; smart emobility, including ebikes, escooters, and kinetic tech. It also provides bags, luggage, travel gears, and wearable accessories under the Vero, Predator, Xplova, and Acer Revivify brands. The company was incorporated in 1975 and is based in New Taipei City, Taiwan. Acer Gadget Inc. is a subsidiary of Acer Inc.
