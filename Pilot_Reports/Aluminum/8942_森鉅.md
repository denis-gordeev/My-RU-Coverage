Xxentria Technology Materials Co., Ltd. manufactures and sells steel composite materials in the United States, Asia, and internationally. The company offers metal products, including laminated, lacquer, PVD coating, and anodizing for exterior facades cladding and interior furnishing for new buildings, as well as refurbishment applications; solar receiver tube and reflective materials for concentrated solar power, residential and industrial solar thermal, cooling, heating, and desalination. It also provides galvanized steel composite panels and high tensile aluminum composite panels for composite wall panels and door panels for truck and trailer bodies. In addition, the company is involved in manufacturing and selling of electronic components; international trade business; pollution control, recycling materials, and waste clearing; investment activities; manufacture and assemble of axles; and operations asset management. Xxentria Technology Materials Co., Ltd. was formally known as Xxentria Industrial Co., Ltd. and changed its name to Xxentria Technology Materials Co., Ltd. in May 2000. The company was founded in 1983 and is headquartered in Tainan City, Taiwan.
