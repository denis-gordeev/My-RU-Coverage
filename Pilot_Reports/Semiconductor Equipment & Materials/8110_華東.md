## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Semiconductor Equipment & Materials
**市值:** 31,002 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 30,444 百萬台幣

Walton Advanced Engineering, Inc., together with its subsidiaries, provides semiconductor packaging and testing services in Taiwan and China. The company offers IC package solutions, including legacy lead frame, wire bond BGA, multichip, and system-in-package. It also provides IC testing services, such as testers, burn-in ovens, and performing wafer probing; testing platform, including chip probing, burn-in, final test, and backend; assembly services; and turnkey and drop shipment services. In addition, the company engages in the design, manufacture, and sale of IC and IC equipment; and general investment activities. The company was formerly known as Walsin Advanced Engineering, Inc. and changed its name to Walton Advanced Engineering, Inc. in July 2002. Walton Advanced Engineering, Inc. was founded in 1995 and is headquartered in Kaohsiung City, Taiwan.
