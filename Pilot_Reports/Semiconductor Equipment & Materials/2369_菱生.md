**產業:** Semiconductor Equipment & Materials
**市值:** 9,455 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 9,394 百萬台幣

Lingsen Precision Industries, Ltd. engages in the semiconductor business in Taiwan, rest of Asia, Europe, the Americas, and Africa. The company is involved in assembly and testing of various integrated circuit and optoelectronics products; and testing, manufacture, packaging, and trading of various semiconductors. It offers assembly services, including reliability test, failure analysis, package characterization, and product services; and test process services for water test and final test equipment. The company was founded in 1970 and is headquartered in Taichung, Taiwan.
