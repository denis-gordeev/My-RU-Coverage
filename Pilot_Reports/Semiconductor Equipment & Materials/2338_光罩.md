## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Semiconductor Equipment & Materials
**市值:** 9,440 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 17,828 百萬台幣

Taiwan Mask Corporation engages in the research, development, manufacturing, and sales of photomasks and integrated circuits for the semiconductor industry. The company provides technical assistance, consultation, inspection, and repair services; precious metal coating; 3D printing and plastic mold design; and packaging and testing services. It also manufactures and sells medical equipment; power component design; and distributes electronic materials and precision equipment, as well as engages in retail and wholesale of medical equipment. In addition, the company is involved in providing electronic parts and components, energy technical services; manufacturing and distribution of electronics components, electronics materials, and precision equipment; and designing of power component. It has operations in Taiwan, Asia, and internationally. Taiwan Mask Corporation was incorporated in 1988 and is headquartered in Hsinchu City, Taiwan.
