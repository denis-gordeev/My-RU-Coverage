**產業:** Semiconductor Equipment & Materials
**市值:** 15,664 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 17,959 百萬台幣

SDI Corporation, together with its subsidiaries, manufactures and sells semiconductor lead frames, LED lead frames, stationery and office products, and high precision dies in Taiwan, China, Japan, Malaysia, and internationally. It engages in the smelting and rolling of metal strips; manufacturing and processing of electronic components; and international trade business. The company was founded in 1953 and is headquartered in Changhua, Taiwan.
