## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Consumer Cyclical
**產業:** Footwear & Accessories
**市值:** 9,910 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 17,991 百萬台幣

Paiho Shih Holdings Corporation, through its subsidiaries, manufactures and sells touch fastener, webbing, elastic, and jacquard engineered mesh products in China and Vietnam. It offers touch fastener products for use in shoes, garments, raincoats, caps, sporting goods, belts, etc.; elastic products for use in pant straps, jacket lower hems, cuffs, shoe upper clothes, and other applications; and jacquard engineered mesh products for use in shoes, garments, sporting goods, child carts, baby goods, household textiles, etc. The company also provides shoelaces/webbing products that include woven strips and shoelaces for use in accessories of decoration for shoe uppers, eyelets and laces, leathers, rear straps for sport bags, caps, etc. In addition, it engages in the commercial property management, design decoration, planning and consultation, and international investment and trading activities; production and sale of masks and non-woven products; leasing and sale of real estate; and production and marketing of mesh and other fabrics. The company's products are used in medical, transportation, electronics, and home applications. The company was incorporated in 2006 and is headquartered in Grand Cayman, the Cayman Islands. Paiho Shih Holdings Corporation operates as a subsidiary of Paiho Int'l Limited.
