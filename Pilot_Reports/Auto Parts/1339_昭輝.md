**板塊:** Consumer Cyclical
**產業:** Auto Parts
**市值:** 3,050 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 2,802 百萬台幣

Y.C.C. Parts Mfg. Co., Ltd. manufactures and sells automotive plastic parts in North America, Central America, South America, Europe, Asia, and Taiwan. The company offers absorber, air deflector, fan shroud, fender, grille moulding, head lamp mounting panel, brackets, cases, side bumpers, valance, and bumper cover, grille, and moulding. It also offers water tank guard, lower panel trim, and headlight bracket. Y.C.C. Parts Mfg. Co., Ltd. was incorporated in 1986 and is based in Lukang, Taiwan.
