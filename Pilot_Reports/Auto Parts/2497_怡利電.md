E-Lead Electronic Co., Ltd. designs and manufactures electronics devices for automotive industry in Taiwan and internationally. The company provides HUD heads-up display, passenger HUD, windshield HUD, combined HUD, windshield reflection HUD, virtual intelligent holographic assistant, and digital dashboard; driving safety protection products comprising driving assistance system, 2D/3D AVM, anti-collision millimeter, car air purifier, front and rear footage, and blind spot detection products. It also offers USB dual charger, electronic rearview mirror driving recorder, digital virtual 3D rearview mirror. In addition, the company provides LCD touch screen and smartphone linked video player. E-Lead Electronic Co., Ltd. was founded in 1983 and is based in Changhua, Taiwan.
