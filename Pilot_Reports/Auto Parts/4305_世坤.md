Shih-Kuen Plastics Co., Ltd. produces and supplies PVC sheets and vinyl films. It offers double polished PVC sheets, such as super and normal clear, and transparent colored PVC sheets; textured and embossing PVC sheets, including opaque and translucent textured, and semi-transparent PVC sheets; super clear and colored laminated thick PVC sheets; and UV stabilized outdoor, cold crack resistant, and fire flame retardant PVC sheets. The company's products are used for home and office supplies, construction and industrial, outdoor PVC waterproof covers, personal protective products, and medical applications. It also exports its products worldwide. Shih-Kuen Plastics Co., Ltd. was founded in 1986 and is based in Tainan City, Taiwan.
