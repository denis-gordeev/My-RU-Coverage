## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Consumer Cyclical

**企業價值:** 2,283 百萬台幣

Hiroca Holdings Ltd., through its subsidiaries, produces and sells various automotive accessories, electronic plastic parts, plastic hollow box packaging products, and molds in China and internationally. It offers injection molding, surface coating, and cubic painting for automotive interiors; and in-mold decoration and out-mold decoration, leather covering, and knob assembly and decoration products and services for use in automotive armrest consoles, gearboxes, dashboards, doors, seats, and steering wheels. The company also provides injection molding and EPF process solutions for automotive exterior trims; LED technology for use in lighting of automotive interior/exterior trim parts; auto part molds; and casings for consumer electronic products. Hiroca Holdings Ltd. was founded in 1998 and is based in Dongguan, China.
