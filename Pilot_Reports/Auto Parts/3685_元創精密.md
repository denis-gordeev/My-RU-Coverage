## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Consumer Cyclical
**產業:** Auto Parts
**市值:** 2,706 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 3,374 百萬台幣

Tradetool Auto Co., Ltd. manufactures and sells metal stamping parts in Taiwan, China, and internationally. It also offers automotive optics parts, such as the light guide plates, frames, auto motive LENs, automotive lamp assembly, and welding parts, as well as develops and sells related tooling. In addition, the company installs, operates, and maintains self-use renewable energy generation equipment, and related operation and maintenance services. Further, it engages in manufacturing of electronic parts and components; wholesale of materials; product designing; manufacturing, processing, and selling of various auto parts and components; and housing rental business. Additionally, the company provides automotive metal parts, such as metal parts stamping, metal component welding, and stamping die design and manufacturing products. The company was formerly known as Jeng Shiang Precision Ind. Co., Ltd. and changed its name to Tradetool Auto Co., Ltd. in May 2016. Tradetool Auto Co., Ltd. was founded in 1980 and is based in Taichung, Taiwan.
