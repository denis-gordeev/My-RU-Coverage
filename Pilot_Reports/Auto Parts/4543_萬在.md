## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Consumer Cyclical
**產業:** Auto Parts
**市值:** 2,044 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 1,901 百萬台幣

Man Zai Industrial Co., Ltd. engages in the manufacture and sale of air- conditioning products in Asia, the United States, Europe, and internationally. The company offers auto air-con modules, including condenser units, evaporator units, and heater cores; automobile and motorcycle aluminum radiators for cooling systems, such as radiator units and oil coolers; and thermal-electric cooling modules comprising thermal modules for CPU, thermal syphon modules, and liquid cooling modules, as well as water-cooled modules for use in CPU, VGA, LED, and auto-electric cooling modules. It also provides aluminum extrusion products that include fittings/brackets/pipes, racket frames, OPC drums, computer heat sinks, and bicycle frames; heat sinks for commercial air conditioners, cloud servers, electrical and mechanical products, and home appliances; and air-con and refrigeration products. The company was founded in 1984 and is based in Tainan City, Taiwan.
