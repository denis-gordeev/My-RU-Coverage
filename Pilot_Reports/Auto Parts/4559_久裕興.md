# 4559 - 久裕興

## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Consumer Cyclical
**產業:** Auto Parts
**市值:** 468 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 1,000 百萬台幣

Joy Industrial Co., Ltd. engages in the research, development, manufacture, import, export, wholesale, trade, and sale of bicycle parts in Taiwan, China, Europe, and internationally. The company offers bicycle hubs and wheelsets; and carbon fiber front fork and grip products. It is also involved in the manufacture of bicycles, automobiles, and traffic equipment parts and accessories; design, development, and production of carbon fiber clad molds; and production and trading of thermoformed bicycles and related accessories. In addition, the company provides hardware products, traffic equipment motors, and controllers; and precision stamping dies, molds, flywheels, transmission, and other parts. The company was formerly known as Jiuyu Industry Co., Ltd. and changed its name to Joy Industrial Co., Ltd. in January 2010. Joy Industrial Co., Ltd. was incorporated in 1971 and is based in Taichung, Taiwan.
