## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Consumer Cyclical
**產業:** Auto Parts
**市值:** 772 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 2,379 百萬台幣

Jui Li Enterprise Co., Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, engages in producing and selling automobile parts, and wholesaling sports equipment in Taiwan and China. It offers exterior body parts, including hood and lid assemblies, front and rear door SASH, front and rear door assemblies LH, front and rear door assemblies RH, fender LH and RH, body-side LH and RH, roofs with sunroof, and roofs, as well as aluminum ally tail-gate assemblies. The company also provides rails and under-body parts; and chassis and structure parts, such as front and rear sub frames. In addition, it offers aftermarket products, including hoods, fenders, radiator supports, rebars, bumper and plastic brackets, and grilles, as well as motorcycle stamping parts, stamping dies, and jigs. Further, the company is involved in leasing of investment real estate; and offering maintenance services of automotive parts, and electronic parts and their molds, fixtures, and gauges. Jui Li Enterprise Co., Ltd. was founded in 1959 and is headquartered in Kaohsiung, Taiwan.
