Chian Hsing Forging Industrial Co., Ltd. engages in the manufacture and sale of various forged products in Taiwan. Its forged products include hardware, bicycle, motorbike, automobile, and track vehicle parts, as well as industrial engineering vehicle components and other products. The company provides auto parts comprising C.V. joints and driving elements of front wheel; inner race crosses; bearing cups, pistons, and housings; transmission parts; transmission bearing spang products; transmission joint gears; mandrils, rear axis, and core axis of the generator; air conditioning belt pulleys; bevel gears; flang outer race; magnetic poles and driving gears; motor brake parts; and aluminum alloy forging parts that include ammonia cans for auto air-conditioners. It also offers assorted hardware parts, including impactors, saddles, pneumatic toolings, tone hole parts, disc knife, and rod couplings, as well as parts of sports goods, which include cranks, high-pressure joints, high-tension bolts/nuts, alloy bearing forgings, and tube forgings. In addition, the company provides motorcycle parts, such as gear shafts, gear blanks, start gears, transmission shafts, and ammonia cans for auto air-conditioners; starting gear and clutch gear forged work products; shaft and axle parts; and clutches, tube yokes, and chain gears. Further, it provides rail parts, such as tie plates, as well as bicycle parts, such as gear wheels. The company was formerly known as Hsieh Hsing Iron Factory and changed its name to Chian Hsing Forging Industrial Co., Ltd. in 1980. Chian Hsing Forging Industrial Co., Ltd. was founded in 1971 and is based in Taichung, Taiwan.
