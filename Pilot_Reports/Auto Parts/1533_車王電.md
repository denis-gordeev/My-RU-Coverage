## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Consumer Cyclical
**產業:** Auto Parts
**市值:** 3,040 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 6,983 百萬台幣

Mobiletron Electronics Co.,Ltd. engages in the design, manufacture, and sale of automotive parts in Taiwan. The company offers charging systems, including brush holders, diodes and diode trios, rectifiers, repair circuits, voltage regulators, and others; and ignition systems, such as ignition modules and coils. It also provides ECU/PCM sensors that include ABS/wheel speed, brake pad wear, crank/cam shaft position, DPF, exhaust gas temperature, knock, MAF and Map, oxygen, throttle position, and transmission speed sensors; fuel management products consisting of EGR and idle air control valves; safety and ADAS systems comprising park assist cameras, PDC parking sensors, and tire pressure monitoring systems; and headlight leveling sensors. In addition, the company exports its products. Mobiletron Electronics Co.,Ltd. was founded in 1971 and is based in Taichung, Taiwan.
