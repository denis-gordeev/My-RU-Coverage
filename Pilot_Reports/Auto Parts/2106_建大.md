Kenda Rubber Industrial Co. Ltd. designs, manufactures, and trades in tires in China, the United States, Taiwan, and Vietnam. The company provides automotive, bicycle, powersports, wheelchair, and turf/trailer/specialty. It offers inner tubes and tires for bicycles, scooters, industrial trucks, and cars, as well as various products of carbon fiber. In addition, the company engages in trading and investing activities; manufacturing and selling of wheels and rims; distributing and selling of tires and rims, as well as provision of marketing planning services. The company was incorporated in 1962 and is based in Yuanlin, Taiwan.
