## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Consumer Cyclical
**產業:** Auto Parts
**市值:** 2,408 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 2,422 百萬台幣

C.T.I. Traffic Industries Co., Ltd. manufactures and sells engine parts in Taiwan and internationally. It offers starter batteries, energy storage systems, green power products, and jump starter products. The company also provides DLC piston rings, comprising black diamond piston rings; gasoline piston ring-oil rings; and chrome, nitriding, and phosphate diesel ring-oil rings. In addition, it offers timing kits and components, such as automotive timing kits, car timing guides, timing guides and chains, tensioners, and cam and crank gears; overhaul engine gaskets, including CYL heads, cylinder head gaskets, intake manifolds, exhaust manifolds, pipe manifolds, automotive oil seals, engine oil seals, oil seals, valve covers, oil pans, valve stem seals, and engine valve seals; liner and piston kits; and engine valves, which consist of auto engine valves, engine valve seats, engine bearings, and engine valve guides; and carburetor kits. Further, the company provides compression rings, including hook, step, inside cut, undercut, notch, double keystone, slip-in, and SH and PH FCD ductile compression rings. Its products are used for automotive, agriculture, off-highway, and heavy-duty truck applications. The company was founded in 1973 and is headquartered in Tainan City, Taiwan.
