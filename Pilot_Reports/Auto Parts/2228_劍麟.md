## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Consumer Cyclical

**企業價值:** 6,472 百萬台幣

Iron Force Industrial Co., Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, manufactures and trades in airbag inflators for automotive safety systems in Taiwan and internationally. Its auto parts include airbag inflators and precision metal parts for automotive safety systems; steering columns/shafts and diesel engine cooling pipes; high precision metal tubes for seatbelt retractor/pre-tensioner systems; in house automated machines; and special surface treatments, as well as related components. The company provides OEM and ODM of customized store display fixtures, display systems design, and retail store design. In addition, it offers clothes hangers, logistic services, mannequins, metal fixtures, and management consulting services. Iron Force Industrial Co., Ltd. was founded in 1977 and is headquartered in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
