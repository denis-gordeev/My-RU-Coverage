Harmony Electronics Co. Ltd manufactures and sells quartz crystal products in Taiwan, Thailand, and China. The company provides crystal oscillators, thermistor crystal, crystal resonators, and MEMS microphones. It also engages in the trading, export, and import activities, and production and operation of new electronic components, as well as investments in various manufacturing businesses and banks. The company serves industrial and networking, data communication, consumer devices, systems, [[5G]] infrastructure, Metaverse, drone, server, and automotive sectors. Harmony Electronics Co. Ltd was incorporated in 1976 and is headquartered in Kaohsiung, Taiwan.
