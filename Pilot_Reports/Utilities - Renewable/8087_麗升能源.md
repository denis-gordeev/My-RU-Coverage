## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Utilities
**產業:** Utilities - Renewable
**市值:** 1,062 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 769 百萬台幣

LeadSun Greentech Corporation, together with its subsidiaries, engages in the construction, development, and sale of power plants. It is also involved in the operation of renewable energy power plant development and planning consultation; investment; and investment management consultation businesses. In addition, the company provides crop cultivation, agricultural retail, and energy technology services; and power generation for self-usage using renewable energy services, as well as solar photovoltaic and wind power generation services. LeadSun Greentech Corporation was formerly known as Homenema Technology Incorporation and changed its name to LeadSun Greentech Corporation in May 2024. The company was founded in 1986 and is based in Taipei, Taiwan.
