**產業:** Security & Protection Services

**企業價值:** 1,223 百萬台幣

Everspring Industry Co., Ltd. engages in the manufacturing, reprocessing, and trading of burglar alarm and other electronic products and parts. The company offers disaster prevention and security solutions; lighting control equipment and accessories of burglar alarms; and intelligent security equipment. It is also involved in research, development, manufacturing, and trading of intelligent security equipment; developing, managing, maintaining, and leasing of residential buildings, as well as trades in real estate properties. Everspring Industry Co., Ltd. was oncorporated in 1980 and is based in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
