## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Industrials
**產業:** Security & Protection Services
**市值:** 15,772 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 10,997 百萬台幣

Taiwan Shin Kong Security Co., Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, provides electronic security services in Taiwan. It operates through Electronic Services Business, Cash-in-transit Business, Stationed Security Business, and Other segments. The company engages in the design, sale, rental, installation, maintenance, repair, and inspection of fire and burglary prevention, and fire protection apparatus; and import and export of related products, as well as operation and investment of relevant businesses. It also provides cash escort and stationed security guard services. In addition, the company engages in general investment; transportation and security maintenance of cash and other valuables; management services for apartments and buildings; operation of parking lots; installation and sale of computer equipment and electronic information software; manufacture and sale of security equipment; research and development of service robots, automation products, access controls, and security systems; real estate leasing; and management, building management, and medical management consulting services. Further, it is involved in information processing; security equipment engineering and construction; food and beverage; internet communication; building information modeling software; retail of medical equipment; car leasing; agency for health care products and technological home appliances; and computer system integration activities. Taiwan Shin Kong Security Co., Ltd. was founded in 1969 and is based in Taipei City, Taiwan.
