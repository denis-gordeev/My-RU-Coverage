## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Industrials
**產業:** Metal Fabrication
**市值:** 31,109 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 46,874 百萬台幣

Century Iron and Steel Industrial Co.,Ltd. engages in the manufacture and sale of steel structures, steel frame, and iron bone in Taiwan and Myanmar. The company is also involved in the manufacture and distributes mechanical equipment of power generation, and fabricated metal; installation; and consulting services. Its products are used in high-rise buildings, bridges, factories, public construction, and other projects. Century Iron and Steel Industrial Co.,Ltd. was incorporated in 1987 and is headquartered in Taoyuan City, Taiwan.
