## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Basic Materials
**產業:** Building Materials
**市值:** 136,824 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 154,611 百萬台幣

Taiwan Glass Ind. Corp. manufactures, processes, and sells various glass products in Taiwan, China, and internationally. The company operates in three segments: Flat Glass, Glass Container, and Glass Fiber. The company offers float and processed glass; auto glass; ultra-thin glass; photovoltaic cover glass; and fabric and fiber glass. It also provides liquor, wine and beer, food jar, pepper, spice, sauce, oil and vinegar, soft drink, juice and milk, pharmaceutical, and cobalt blue containers; tableware, such as bowls and plates, water tumblers, wine glasses, mugs, storage jars and boxes, ashtrays, candleholders, and others; and kitchenware comprising tea pots, pitchers, bubble balls, storage jars, coffee pots, and glass covers. Taiwan Glass Ind. Corp. was incorporated in 1964 and is based in Taipei, Taiwan.
