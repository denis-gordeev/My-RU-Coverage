Wiselink Co., Ltd. manufactures and sells zippers under the MAX Zipper brand name worldwide. It offers invisible, coil, Delrin, and metal zippers and sliders; tape attachable and attach links, spring-lock, and plastic slider; and metal, plastic, cord, diamante, leather, and woven puller. The company's products are used in garment, sporting goods, leather bags, luggage, automobile seats cover, and other processed goods. Wiselink Co., Ltd. was founded in 1978 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
